Who we are

We want to make life easier for everyone.

WonderBill was founded in 2015 to help you manage your bills and make smart switching decisions. Shell Technology Ventures and BCG Digital Ventures think we’re doing such a good job that they’ve backed us. Our team’s grown massively in the last year and we plan to keep getting bigger.

What we offer

We can’t promise cake everyday (only on special occasions) but we can promise an exciting environment where you’ll be able to help us build new products. All of our departments are responsible for making WonderBill excellent and that means we share a lot of work and we’re not afraid to try new things. If you love a challenge, or if you’re really passionate about building engaging customer experiences you might be an excellent fit.
