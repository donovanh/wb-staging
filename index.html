How we use your data

We use a number of security systems in order to protect your information. Our service was designed with your safety and privacy in mind.

Account linking

In order to host your bills we ask for your provider login details. We have read-only access, so your account is still only operated by you.

Data protection

Your data is safe with us. We’ll never share it with third parties without your permission and we won’t sell it.

FAQs

How do I get started?

Sign up to WonderBill in seconds and start adding your providers to your accounts to view your bills.

How much does it cost?

WonderBill is completely free and there are no hidden costs.

Will WonderBill pay my bills for me?

You can use your account to view your bills and PDF statements but payments have to be made outside our service.

At the moment we support over 40 providers and we’re always looking to add new ones.
