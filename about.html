About Us

We're here to simplify your household bills.

WonderBill was created to make bill management easier:

We want to give you access to all of your statements and bill PDFs on the go and help you make smarter decisions.

Mission

Our goal is to make sure you’re in total control of your household bills — It’s easy to get lost in paper bills and different provider websites, so we created a better way.

Our Journey

“We started in late 2015 as a collaboration between Shell Technology Ventures and BCG Digital Ventures to create an easy to use platform for managing your monthly bills and finding better deals. Since then we’ve released our app on Android and iOS, thousands of users have joined us and we’re only just getting started.”

Shane Clifford
